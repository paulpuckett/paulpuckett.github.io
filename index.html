Power Plant Locater

A map used to locate coal, natural gas, and hydro plants across the U.S.A.

Click an area to isolate power plants in that area, double-click to refine a narrower area, and click on the stations themselves for more information

  • See the map: Power Plant Locater
  • Data Source: Bureau of Labor Statistics
  • See the code for this map

Kentucky Vacant Housing Stock

A map used to identify vacant housing stock, by county, in Kentucky

Use the pulldown menu to identify different facets of vacant housing stock in Kentucky, mouse over counties for more info, and click on an individual county for more information.

  • See the map: Vacant Housing Stock in KY
  • Data Source: Bureau of Labor Statistics
  • See the code for this map
