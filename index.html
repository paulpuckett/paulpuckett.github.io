Services and Access Map: NE Lexington KY

A collaborative map, combining the efforts of students in GEO409/509 to analyze modal inequities and access to daily services in northeast Lexington.

Click a starting location, then click on a service to learn the travel times to that service on foot, on bike, or in a car. Double-click to see the extents to which the average pedestrian can travel in ten minutes, and consider the opportunities available or unavailable to those with income insufficient to support an automobile.

  • See the map: Services and Access Map
  • Data Source: Data compiled, processed, and map authored by Koenigsmark, Lynn, and Puckett
  • See the code for this map
